About Us

About CNC Consultancy

CNC Consultancy is a Nepal-based Civil Engineering and Information Technology consultancy firm dedicated to delivering innovative infrastructure and digital solutions. We combine engineering expertise with cutting-edge technology to support sustainable development and business growth across Nepal.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality engineering and technology solutions that contribute to Nepal's infrastructure and digital transformation.

Our Vision

To become one of Nepal's leading consultancy firms in Civil Engineering, IT Development, Robotics, and AI Innovation.
